Heyy!!! I'm Jo — a 21-year-old girl with a PhD in daydreaming and a brain full of thoughts I never finish.

This diary? A space for random moments that mean something to me. Nothing fancy, just me.

I constantly get into trouble — not because of drama, but because my mouth runs on 4G while my brain is still buffering. I talk to strangers like we’re long-lost besties. But like Bluetooth on a bus, the bond drops the moment I move.

Whatever I feel, I need that perfect cup of tea.

Even extroverts have their silent era. When something hurts, I go full movie mode — window staring, sad playlist on max, blabbering to the moon like I’m auditioning for a reality show no one asked for.

Listening? Not really. Talking? Absolutely.

My brain is a theater playing random creative ideas all day — will I do anything about them? Nope. Lazy is my middle name.
